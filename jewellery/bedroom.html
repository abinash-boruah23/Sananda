Bedroom Design

Aim of this project is to design a custom minimalist bedroom for a young professional client. The design incorporates the key elements of minimalism while adding touches of the client's preferred accent colors in the bedding, artwork, and accessories. It's customized to their needs and preferences, including storage solutions and lighting options, with functionality in mind. Deliverables include a 3D visualization, detailed floor plan, recommended furnishings, and a color palette and styling guide.
