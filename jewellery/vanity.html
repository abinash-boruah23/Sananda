Vanity Tray

Designing a minimalist-inspired vanity tray using faux marble, wood, and mirror, while featuring clean lines and subtle curves. Incorporating a muted color palette of white, soft pink and beige, the overall design looks simple yet sophisticated, combining the elegance of marble and mirror with the warmth of wood. The legs feature intersecting curves made of rose gold metal, adding a luxurious touch to the overall design. Ensuring functionality, the tray can hold a variety of vanity items while maintaining a minimalist aesthetic.
