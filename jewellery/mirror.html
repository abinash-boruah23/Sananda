Mirage Mirror

The primary source of inspiration for this bathroom mirror design is the unique and intricate textures found in sand dunes. These natural formations embody a sense of harmony, serenity, and fluidity that we aim to capture in the mirror ' s design. The ripple patterns, soft curves, and gradients seen in sand dunes are the foundation for the mirror ' s concept creating a visual and tactile connection with these natural wonders, allowing users to bring a piece of nature into their daily routine
