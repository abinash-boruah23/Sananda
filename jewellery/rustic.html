Rustic Radiance

Introducing a collection of lights for Opaque Studio, a brand that specializes in handcrafted furniture and home decor products made using natural and sustainable materials. . Launching a collection of lamps named "Rustic Radiance" which will be an add on to the brand's existing product line taking inspiration from Japandi style which is a mixture of Japanese and Scandinivian style. The materials used in the designs will be natural and sustainable which is in line with the brand's primary objective.
